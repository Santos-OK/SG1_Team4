09 — Strategy Pitch

The Investment Case

Three energy management strategies were simulated. The data tells a clear story for where to focus capital deployment.

💡 Key Finding #1
Studios = Best ROI

Low-income studios achieve 95% self-sufficiency. They earn revenue by exporting excess solar to the grid — becoming micro power plants.

⚡ Key Finding #2
Battery = Multiplier

Households with a Tesla Powerwall bridge the 6-hour solar gap. Without it, luxury homes spend 10× more on grid imports per month.

🎯 Key Finding #3
Target Large Families

Large families have the highest absolute savings potential. A solar+battery package could cut their grid bill by 60–70%, saving $800+/month.

Household Net Grid Cost (30 days) — lower is better
The verdict: Studios with solar actually earn money (negative cost = revenue). Luxury homes without battery storage hemorrhage cash to the utility. The solution is not "more solar" — it's smarter storage paired with the right household profile.
